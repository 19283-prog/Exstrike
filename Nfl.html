Code Cracker AI

Browser-based cipher helper for puzzles and learning. Now with typo cleanup, context-aware scoring, and a small built-in chat helper.

This is a heuristic puzzle tool. It cannot truly crack every code, but it can rank likely plaintext and use your context to improve guesses.
